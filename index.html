  • Democratizing LLMs

    Large Language Models (LLMs) are some of the most sophisticated AI systems ever created, capable of understanding and processing vast amounts of language-based data. They have revolutionized the field of natural language processing and have a wide range of applications, from chatbots and virtual assistants to language translation and content creation.

  • AI Safety and Aligment

    Artificial intelligence (AI) is rapidly advancing, and as it does, so do concerns about AI safety and alignment. AI safety refers to the potential risks and negative consequences that could arise from the development and deployment of advanced AI systems. AI alignment, on the other hand, refers to the challenge of designing AI systems that share human values and goals.

  • Research

    In order to build LLMs, we need to conduct extensive research in several areas to create high-quality models. LLMs are complex neural networks that can process vast amounts of text data, understand its meaning, and generate human-like language output. At Chatbotics we have developed the infrastructure and expertise to develop in-house all the steps required to build such models from scratch.
